Classic Cheeseburger

A timeless favorite, the Classic Cheeseburger combines a juicy beef patty, melted cheese, and fresh toppings, all nestled in a soft, toasted bun. Perfect for a casual dinner or a weekend barbecue, this cheeseburger is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingridents

  • 1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Pickle slices
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • Steps
    1. Prepare the Patties:In a large bowl, combine the ground beef with salt and pepper. Mix gently to avoid overworking the meat. ,Divide the meat into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty about 3/4 inch thick.
    2. Preheat the Grill or Pan:Preheat your grill to medium-high heat or heat a large skillet over medium-high heat on the stove.
    3. Cook the Patties:Place the patties on the grill or in the skillet. Cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness. ,During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty to melt.
    4. Toast the Buns:While the patties are cooking, butter the insides of the hamburger buns. ,Toast the buns on the grill or in the skillet until golden brown.
    5. Assemble the Burgers:Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the bottom half of each bun. ,Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom bun, followed by a slice of tomato, onion, and pickle slices. ,Add the cooked patty with melted cheese on top of the vegetables. and Cover with the top half of the bun..
